Steps:

  1. Define generative model of the sample

    • We have 4 variables:

      • p: proportion of water (estimand)
      • N: number of tosses
      • W: water observations
      • L: land observations

    • Influences:

      • N->W ; N->L; p->W; p->L

      • W,L = f(p,N)

  2. Define a specific estimand

    • p

  3. Design a statistical way to produce estimand

    • W,L = (4p)W + (4-4p)L

      sample <- c("W","L","W","W","W","L","W","L","W")

L <- sum(sample=="L")
